Discover Captain

What's Captain?

It's an ever lasting memory of all your web application's releases.

It has lots of different tools that will help you analyze your deployments history.

How does it work?

Captain is a combination of a simple but effective gem, plus a centralized Data Management System.

It seamlessly integrates into your application's routines and gather data in background.

Trust your Captain

Here's what it's all about. Super features for your awesome crew!

Magix Auto

is a powerful custom syntax you'll learn to use in your pull requests, commits and issues.

With the right amount of data it'll come out with a wonderful and automated changelog of your application.

Paperwork

is an awesome, totally customizable and automated newsletter that will keep your crew always up-to-date.

Customized messages and email layouts. Deployment's report and changelog. Feedback requests and a lot more coming.

Time Machine

is a simple but effective time capsule lifeline for your application's deployments.

Manage rollbacks, tags and releases in a snap. Always know what you added and what you removed. It's simply awesome!
