Meet Captain

What's Captain?

It's a beautifully organized comprehensible history of all your deployments.

It is designed to make everyone understand what's going on into your app,
not only developers.

What does it do?

It tracks and organizes changes
to your app into a single but
effective status page.

Why do you need it?

It keeps everyone in your team on
the same page and engaged with
the development process.

Who's it for?

Captain's for you, the boss, and every member of your organization.
Either small or large teams.

How does it work, exactly?


You deploy your app

Whatever system you're using
there's an integration for that.

Captain gets notified

The database gets what you're doing.
The timeline is updated.

Your team gets notified

Email, HipChat, Slack everthing that you need to inform your team.

Kickass feature-set

Here's what it's all about


Timeline and notifications

Captain keeps record of all your deployments in his smart timeline. It also notifies your team when something's changed.


Bug and feedback management system

Let your team report bugs and share your thoughts
on your current release.
They'll get notified once a bug goes to production too!


Connected apps

Link external apps to perform actions when a deployment is made. Manage them through your backend right on Captain.
